What You Can Do

Your membership and donation to the Cleveland National Forest Foundation will be used to achieve our goals to preserve and protect our forest wilderness.

All funding received and administered by the Foundation will be used to acquire and protect land in or near the forest that is threatened by development. Funds also will be used for special projects selected by our members, such as the reintroduction of native species to the forest.
